Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

How do I download the Ritmo app? +

You can download Ritmo from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Search for "Ritmo" and tap the download button. Once installed, open the app and create your account with your email.

Which wearables does Ritmo support? +

Ritmo works with most popular fitness trackers and smartwatches including Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, and Samsung Galaxy Watch.

Do I need to pay for Ritmo? +

Ritmo is completely free for all users. We don't charge membership fees or require in-app purchases. Just download the app and start running!

Can I use Ritmo without a smartwatch? +

Yes! Ritmo works great without a wearable. You can use your phone's GPS to track your run, though a wearable device provides more detailed metrics.

How does Ritmo personalize my music? +

Ritmo uses AI to analyze your running pace and creates playlists with songs that match your BPM. The music adapts in real-time as you speed up or slow down.

Is my data safe with Ritmo? +

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal data. Ritmo is built with privacy in mind.
